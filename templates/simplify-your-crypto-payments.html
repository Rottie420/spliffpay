Your Questions Answered

Why use crypto payments for your business?

Choosing to accept cryptocurrency payments can offer your business lower fees, faster transactions, greater security, and access to a global market. It also positions your brand as innovative and customer-centric, catering to the growing number of consumers who value and use digital currencies.

Why you should use spliffpay?

Spliffpay is an alternative payment solution that helps merchant accept cryptocurrency as payment. Whether you are a small business owner or a large enterprise, SpliffPay’s flexible and scalable solutions can be tailored to meet your specific needs, helping you stay ahead in the competitive market.

What tokens are supported by spliffpay?

Spliffpay is currently supporting ERC-20 tokens this includes $ETH , $SHIB, $USDT, $MATIC, $LINK, $AXS. we will be adding more tokens in the future updates.
