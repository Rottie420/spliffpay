The integration is facilitated by connecting Spliffpay with MetaMask, the leading Ethereum wallet extension. By leveraging the MetaMask API, Spliffpay ensures a smooth and intuitive experience for businesses, allowing users to make payments directly from their MetaMask wallets. This integration not only streamlines the process but also opens the door to the future of digital payments, making Spliffpay an ideal choice for businesses ready to embrace the potential of ERC-20 tokens and the evolving landscape of cryptocurrency transactions.
